# Vantage: The Bridge Between Eras

**Vantage** is an intergenerational skill-trading platform that connects the wisdom of seniors with the technical fluency of youth through a time-bank economy. It moves beyond the "lonely senior" trope to build a **knowledge equity** ecosystem rooted in reciprocity, not charity.

## The Concept in 30 Seconds

- **Problem:** Gen Z/Alpha feel economically stuck and lack practical life skills. Seniors feel socially invisible and lack digital fluency.
- **Solution:** A platform where **Time is the Currency** — seniors teach life skills, youth teach technology, and both earn **V-Credits** redeemable within the ecosystem.
- **Differentiator:** AI-assisted temperament matching, dual-mode UI ("Classic" for seniors, "Pulse" for youth), and a blockchain-secured time-bank ledger.

---

## Core Pillars

1. **Value for Value** — no charity dynamic; every exchange is mutual
2. **Legacy Preservation** — AI-assisted "Story-Nodes" archive senior wisdom as shareable video clips
3. **Safe by Design** — ID.me verification, Guardian Mode for family oversight, and WebRTC for private video calls
